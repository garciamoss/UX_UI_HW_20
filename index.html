Overview

Students were placed into several groups charged with redesigning a website from a list of federal government agencies; my group chose the Department of Labor. During the five weeks to complete the assignment, students learned and applied UI fundamentals and best practices such as usability & accessibility heuristics, UI systems & navigation, and UI & A/B Testings. Deliverables included documentation of various UI research methods during the assignment’s discovery phase, a style guide ensuring consistency of various branding components, and high-fidelity clickable mobile and desktop prototypes (and iterations).

About DOL

The United States Department of Labor is one of the executive departments of the U.S. federal government. Its mission is to foster, promote, and develop the welfare of the wage earners, job seekers, and retirees of the United States; improve working conditions; advance opportunities for profitable employment; and assure work-related benefits and rights. It is headed by the Secretary of Labor, who reports directly to the President and is a member of the president's Cabinet.

Formed: 1913
Website: www.dol.gov
